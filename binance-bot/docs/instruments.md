## Cryptocurrency

### Description
Digital assets secured by cryptography, decentralized, traded on crypto exchanges.

### Characteristics
- **24/7 market** - Always trading
- **High volatility** - Large price swings
- **High liquidity (major pairs)** - Easy to enter/exit
- **Low barriers** - No KYC on many exchanges
- **Fractional** - Can buy tiny amounts

### Popular Assets
| Asset | Symbol | Market Cap | Volatility |
|-------|--------|------------|------------|
| Bitcoin | BTC | ~$1T | Medium |
| Ethereum | ETH | ~$300B | Medium-High |
| Solana | SOL | ~$50B | High |
| Binance Coin | BNB | ~$70B | Medium |
| Stablecoins | USDT, USDC | N/A | Low |

### Exchanges
- Binance (largest volume)
- Bybit
- OKX
- Kraken
- Coinbase

### Pros
- 24/7 trading
- High volatility = high profit potential
- Low entry barrier
- Decentralized
- Many pairs to trade

### Cons
- Regulatory risk
- High volatility = high loss potential
- Exchange risk (hack, insolvency)
- Complex tax reporting

### Risk Level: High
### Best for: Aggressive traders, tech-savvy

## Stocks

### Description
Ownership shares in publicly traded companies.

### Characteristics
- **Market hours** - Typically 9:30-16:00 local time
- **Lower volatility** - More stable than crypto
- **Fundamental analysis** - News, earnings matter
- **Regulation** - Heavily regulated
- **Dividends** - Some pay income

### Popular Markets
| Market | Hours | Example |
|--------|-------|---------|
| US | 9:30-16:00 EST | AAPL, TSLA, NVDA |
| Europe | 9:00-17:30 CET | SAP, ASML |
| Asia | Varies | 9984.T (SoftBank) |

### Brokers
- Interactive Brokers
- Fidelity
- Charles Schwab
- Robinhood
- eToro

### Pros
- Stable companies
- Dividends
- Fundamental analysis
- Regulatory protection

### Cons
- Limited hours
- Less volatile
- Pattern day trader rules
- Higher fees

### Risk Level: Medium
### Best for: Long-term investors, fundamentals-focused

## Forex (Foreign Exchange)

### Description
Trading currency pairs, e.g., EUR/USD.

### Characteristics
- **24/5 market** - Monday-Friday
- **High liquidity** - Largest market in world
- **Leverage** - Trade larger positions
- **Low spreads** - Especially major pairs
- **Macro-driven** - News, interest rates

### Popular Pairs
| Pair | Name | Volatility |
|------|------|------------|
| EUR/USD | Euro/US Dollar | Low |
| GBP/USD | Cable | Medium |
| USD/JPY | Yen | Low-Medium |
| USD/CHF | Swissy | Low |
| AUD/USD | Aussie | Medium |
| USD/CAD | Loonie | Medium |
| NZD/USD | Kiwi | Medium |

### Brokers
- OANDA
- IG Markets
- FOREX.com
- TD Ameritrade
- Interactive Brokers

### Pros
- Highest liquidity
- 24/5 trading
- Low spreads
- High leverage
- No exchange risk

### Cons
- Central bank intervention
- Complex macro factors
- Leverage = fast losses
- Many scam brokers

### Risk Level: High (due to leverage)
### Best for: Technical traders, news traders

## Commodities

### Description
Physical goods traded on markets: metals, energy, agriculture.

### Categories
| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Precious Metals | Gold (XAU), Silver (XAG) |
| Energy | Crude Oil (WTI, Brent), Natural Gas |
| Agriculture | Wheat, Corn, Coffee, Sugar |
| Livestock | Cattle, Hogs |

### Trading Methods
- Futures contracts
- ETFs
- CFDs
- Physical (not practical)

### Popular Commodities
| Commodity | Symbol | Exchange |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Gold | GC | COMEX |
| Silver | SI | COMEX |
| Crude Oil | CL | NYMEX |
| Brent | BZ | ICE |
| Natural Gas | NG | NYMEX |

### Pros
- Inflation hedge
- Diversification
- Some have clear patterns
- Physical demand

### Cons
- Storage issues (physical)
- Complex futures markets
- Seasonal patterns
- Weather dependent (agriculture)

### Risk Level: Medium-High
### Best for: Diversification, inflation hedging

## Indices

### Description
Track performance of group of stocks, representing a market segment.

### Major Indices
| Index | Country | Components |
|-------|---------|------------|
| S&P 500 | US | Top 500 US companies |
| Dow Jones | US | 30 major companies |
| NASDAQ | US | Tech-heavy |
| DAX | Germany | 40 major |
| Nikkei 225 | Japan | 225 companies |
| FTSE 100 | UK | 100 companies |

### Trading Methods
- Index futures
- ETFs (SPY, QQQ)
- CFDs
- Index funds

### Pros
- Diversification built-in
- Market direction plays
- Lower individual stock risk
- Clear benchmarks

### Cons
- Can't pick "winners"
- Correlation during crashes
- Expensive leverage
- Less excitement

### Risk Level: Medium
### Best for: Passive investors, market direction plays

## ETFs (Exchange-Traded Funds)

### Description
Basket of assets that trades like a stock.

### Types
| Type | Examples | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Stock ETFs | SPY, VOO | Track indices |
| Bond ETFs | AGG | Track bonds |
| Sector ETFs | XLF (financials) | Sector exposure |
| Commodity ETFs | GLD (gold) | Commodity exposure |
| Crypto ETFs | IBIT | Bitcoin exposure |
| Inverse | SH | Short market |

### Pros
- Instant diversification
- Low fees
- Trade like stocks
- Tax efficient
- Many strategies available

### Cons
- Management fees
- Tracking error
- Liquidity varies
- Can't customize

### Risk Level: Low-Medium (depends on type)
### Best for: Beginners, long-term investors

## Options

### Description
Contracts giving right (not obligation) to buy/sell asset at set price.

### Types
| Type | Direction | When Exercised |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| Call | Buy | If price goes up |
| Put | Sell | If price goes down |

### Strategies
- Covered calls (income)
- Protective puts (insurance)
- Spreads (risk reduction)
- Straddles (volatility plays)

### Pros
- Limited risk
- Leverage
- Income generation
- Hedging

### Cons
- Complex
- Time decay
- Liquidity issues
- Requires experience

### Risk Level: High (can lose 100%)
### Best for: Advanced traders, hedgers

## Key Considerations

1. **Time availability** - 24/7 vs market hours
2. **Capital** - Some require more to start
3. **Risk tolerance** - Match strategy to comfort
4. **Knowledge** - Start with what you understand
5. **Regulation** - Your jurisdiction matters
6. **Tax implications** - Varies by instrument
7. **Broker/exchange availability** - Must be accessible
